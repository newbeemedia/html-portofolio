Top Movies of All Time According to Eric

My top 3 of all time movies

Avengers: Endgame

Avengers: Endgame is the epic conclusion to a decade-long storyline in the Marvel Cinematic Universe. It follows the aftermath of Thanos' devastating snap in Infinity War, as the remaining Avengers attempt to undo his actions and bring back their fallen comrades. The film is packed with action, emotion, and nostalgia, bringing together almost every major character from the MCU for an unforgettable finale.

Spider-Man: No Way Home

Spider-Man: No Way Home is a groundbreaking film that brings together three generations of Spider-Man on the big screen. After his identity is revealed, Peter Parker turns to Doctor Strange for help, but a spell goes awry, causing villains from other universes to appear. This film is a must-see for any Spider-Man fan, as it explores themes of identity, responsibility, and the multiverse.

Law Abiding Citizen

Law Abiding Citizen is a dark and gritty thriller that explores the concept of vigilante justice. After his family is brutally murdered, Clyde Shelton becomes disillusioned with the criminal justice system and decides to take matters into his own hands. The film delves into moral gray areas and raises questions about the nature of justice and revenge.
